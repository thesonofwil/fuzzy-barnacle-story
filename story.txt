The Ugly Barnacle.

Once there was an ugly barnacle.
He was so ugly that everyone died.
The end. 

The Ugly Barnacle, the Sequel.
Once there was an ugly barnacle. But this time, he wasn't alone.
There were now two ugly barnacles, and sometimes they conversed about their
undersea lives.
The end.

The Ugly Barnacle, the Finale.
Thrice there was an ugly barnacle. Except this time, he wasn't ugly.
He was a beautiful barnacle living in a beautiful sea.
The end.

The Beautiful Barnacle, the True Ending.
Once there was a beautiful barnacle. The barnacle had many friends
in this beautiful sea. And the barnacle said  to himself, 
"This life is a beautiful one." 
The end.
